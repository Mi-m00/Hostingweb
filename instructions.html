Instructions

Preperation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the white, brown sugar, salt and softened butter, until combined.
  3. Next, thourougly whisk in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. In a seperate bowl, combine the AP flour, baking soda, chocolate chunks, and optional add ins.
  5. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet mix with a spatuala, being careful not to overmix.
  6. Chill the cookie dough for at least 30 minutes
  7. Scoop/ shape the dough with spoons onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie so that the cookies can spread evenly.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges have started to barely brown.
  9. Cool completely, and enjoy!
