Ingredients

These cookies are super forgiving, so feel free to add extra mix-ins

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Dry Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups AP flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 oz milk or semi-sweet chocolate chunks
  • Optional: 1 oz oreos
  • Optional: 1 oz caramel bits
